Montar rotas de Gestão de Ativos no app Express (após outras rotas /api):

  const assetManagement = require('./src/routes/assetManagement');
  app.use('/api/asset-management', assetManagement);

Rotas disponíveis (mock):
  GET  /api/asset-management/twins
  GET  /api/asset-management/twins/:id
  POST /api/asset-management/twins/:id/simulate
  GET  /api/asset-management/stock
  PATCH /api/asset-management/stock/:id
  POST /api/asset-management/stock/purchase-order
  GET  /api/asset-management/orders
  POST /api/asset-management/orders/:id/approve
  POST /api/asset-management/orders/:id/reassign
  POST /api/asset-management/orders

Todas as rotas exigem autenticação (Bearer token).
